ABOUT THE BISHOP

Described as a woman of courage, conviction and character - Bishop Carter believes wholeheartedly in the redemptive transformative power of Jesus Christ. A former government official, she serves as the Senior Pastor of the Zion Hill World Harvest Baptist Church, Inc. (Syracuse, New York). Licensed and ordained at the Tucker Missionary Baptist Church by her “Father in Ministry” (The Rev. Dr. Leslie J. Johnson II) - Bishop Carter was consecrated to the office of Bishop in January 2018. A respected woman of faith, entrepreneur and civic leader – Bishop Carter has served in pastoral ministry for over 20 years. A graduate of Syracuse University, Bishop Carter did postgraduate work at Colgate Rochester Divinity School. She is the recipient of numerous leadership awards and salutations including the: New York State Senate “Woman of Achievement”, the Delta Sigma Theta “Marjorie Dowdell Fortitude Award” and the Omega Psi Phi “Citizen of the Year Award”. She serves on a number of boards and is active in the work of social justice within her community. Bishop Carter is a wife, mother, grandmother and spiritual mother to sons & daughters in ministry. One of her favorite scriptures is Philippians 3:14”I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus”

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: 15 Adr street, 5015, NY
